ABOUT ME

Hi.


My name is Michael.


The site you are visiting holds some special memories of mine, containing many of my favorite photograhs. I originally started photography because it seemed like an easy 'A' for a class, an easy hobby, but it quickly grew into something more. In only a few short months, I was eager to explore, capture something new, and spend more money on camera equipment!


I am an amateur photogrpher, and my shots reflect that with their many technical flaws; regardless, I am still proud of my work. Just trying to capture something from a perspective unseen ordinarily, is what interests me the most.


The site is titled A Travelers Tale, due to a 6 month road trip I went on some years ago, most of the photos you will find here will be from that trip. At the time I had a grand plan to share my adventures online, and even bought the domain! Unfortunately I lacked the technical expertise, and the drive to learn how, to create a useful site at the time. However for more than a year, programming and web development have become big interests of mine. Having learned HTML, CSS, and various Javascript frameworks, I figured the time is right to return to the project and give it proper justice.
